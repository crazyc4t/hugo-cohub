Our Services

We assist Business, Owners, Property Managers and End users in attaining to their facility needs and requirements. We do this by providing reliability in every step of our service. Our philosophy stresses the finding of best viable solution while achieving the best quality of work.

CCTV. Security Surveillance.

Closed Circuit Television used for security surveillance for common public access areas, where the presence of these systems will often dissuade possible criminal activity and will keep a record of the areas protected. We offer different types of cameras like dome, bullet, 360 and more.

Access controls. Fobs.

Access control is the management of who goes where and when, keeping track of each person identified with a fob that is allowed specific access on common areas and at specific times. We offer a big variety of key fobs, tags and remotes from the industry leaders like Keyscan, HID, Linear and Mircom, with it's proper installation for everything that your facility needs.

Structured Cabling. Low Voltage.

With our electrician team specialized on low voltage solutions, we cover the installation of access control systems, CCTV cameras, Video Intercoms, and all telephone and data connectivity requirements in the facility, Ensuring proper infrastructure performance of your systems.
